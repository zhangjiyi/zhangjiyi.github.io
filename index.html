About Me

I am a Machine Learning Engineer at TikTok, with a Ph.D. from the National University of Singapore. My work bridges research and industry, with a focus on building robust and secure AI systems. My research interests include diffusion models, large language models, graph-based learning, digital watermarking, and AI security. In the industry, I have applied these areas to real-world systems, contributing to recommendation systems, content moderation, and risk detection.
